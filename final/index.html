ETHAN BLOUIN

Designer

An identity is a crucial element in the pursuit of a business's success. It is my core belief that by creating a friendly and relatable brand for the consumer it is possible for them to establish a relationship with the business. I have experience with branding from preschool programs to start-up breweries.

Print is most certainly still a lively medium, and will continue to be such well into the digital age. I enjoy having a part in every part of the production process, from designing to paper choice and assemblage. I have experience with book, package, and collateral design.

I was taught how to use a camera at an early age by by grandparents, both of which are professional photographers. I enjoy shooting freelance portraiture, but also have an affinity for in-the-moment photojournalism.
