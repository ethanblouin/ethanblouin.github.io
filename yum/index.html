LEMON GRASS –&– CHICKEN ROLLS

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 2 tbs minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 tbs minced fresh jalapeno chile
  • ¼ c minced fresh Thai basil leaves
  • ¼ c minced fresh mint leaves
  • ¼ c minced fresh cilantro
  • ½ c ground peanuts
  • ¼ c fish sauce
  • 3 tbs lime juice
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 1 tbs sesame oil
  • 1 tbs peanut oil
  • 16 rice paper wrappers
  • 16 leaves, red leaf lettuce

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly-salted water and cook in the boiling water until no longer pink in the center, about 7-10 min. Transfer to a large platter and allow to cool completely in refrigerator. Shred into small pieces.
  2. Combine shredded chicken, ginger, jalapeno pepper, cucumber, basil, mint, cilantro, lemon grass, and peanuts in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add to chicken mixture and mix with hands until evenly coated.
  4. Fill a shallow pan with hot water. Dip rice paper wrappers in until soft one at a time. Spread rice paper onto a clean, flat surface. Place 1 leaf of lettuce into the center of a sheet of rice paper; spread about ¹/³ c chicken mixture onto the lettuce leaf. Fold bottom end of rice paper over the top of mixture and roll into a cylinder. Cut into halves to serve.
